Investor Guide

Frequently
Asked Questions

Can foreigners buy property in Dubai? +
Yes. Dubai allows 100% foreign ownership in designated freehold areas. As a non-resident, you can own property outright with full title deed rights — no local sponsor required.
What is an off-plan property? +
Off-plan means purchasing a property before it is built, directly from the developer. These typically offer lower entry prices, flexible payment plans (often 10–20% down), and strong appreciation potential upon completion.
What ROI can I expect in Dubai? +
Dubai offers some of the highest rental yields globally — typically 6–10% annually depending on location and property type. Plus, there is no property tax or capital gains tax in the UAE.
Do I need to be in Dubai to buy? +
No. We assist clients remotely — from virtual property tours to digital document signing and power of attorney arrangements. Many of our clients invest from abroad.
What fees are involved in buying property? +
Typically: 4% Dubai Land Department transfer fee, 2% agency commission (often covered by developer on off-plan), plus registration fees. We provide a full cost breakdown upfront — no surprises.
How does Falcon Edge earn its commission? +
For off-plan properties, our commission is paid by the developer — meaning our services are free for the buyer. For secondary market resales, we work on a transparent agreed fee.
Is Dubai real estate a safe investment? +
Dubai has a highly regulated real estate market governed by RERA (Real Estate Regulatory Agency). Developer escrow accounts, title deed protections, and strong legal frameworks make it one of the most secure markets globally.
